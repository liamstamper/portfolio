SeatStock- coming soon
SeatStock is a dynamic and user-friendly online platform designed to simplify the process of purchasing event tickets for students.
Key Features:
    User Profiles: Users create accounts to Buy and Bid, manage their ticket booking, sell tickets and track their order history
    User-Friendly Interface: The website offers an intuitive and visually appealing user interface, ensuring a seamless experience for students of all technical backgrounds.
    Event Listings: Students can browse a wide range of events with detailed information, including event descriptions, dates, venues, and pricing.
    Event Search and Filtering: Students can easily search for events based on their preferences, such as location, date, type, or artist, streamlining the ticket selection process.
    Email Notifications: Users receive email confirmations and reminders about their booked events, enhancing the overall user experience.
    Responsive Design:The website is designed to be responsive and accessible across various devices, from desktops to mobile phones.
