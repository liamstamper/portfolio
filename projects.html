SeatStock-Lauching November 30th
(No demo is yet provided because Seatstock is on going project)
Seatstock is an online platform meant to permanently simplify the exchange of student tickets to any and all university sporting events. Starting with the University of Iowa (the next step will be expanding to other universities) we are providing a way for students to securely sell any student section ticket they don't plan to use. Not only am I a founder of this project I served as lead frontend engineer for the site. For frontend design I predominantly worked using React and Tailwind CSS. As soon as this project launches (expected date is November 30th) I will upload a video walkthrough and link the site.
